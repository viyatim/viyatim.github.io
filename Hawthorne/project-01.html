PRINT DESIGN PROJECT

This Design Project as the name suggests is all about Prints. Different prints were created keeping the theme - "City of Cuba" in mind. Cuba is a beautiful country that every traveller should visit, perhaps even more than once. Cuban cities include Cienfuegos, Havana, Trinidad, Santiago and many more. The Fortress and the famous beaches at Havana are fascinating and the night view is incredible! Exploring the cobbled streets of Trinidad, admiring its colonial buildings, and taking pictures of the balconies and the beautiful gardens is relaxing. The famous Cuban cigars are totally worth it's name. And lastly, one may also take pictures of the many amazing vintage cars that will be seen parked at traffic lights being polished.
